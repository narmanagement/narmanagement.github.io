Use of Cookies
We use cookies on our Website. By accessing and using our Website, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this Policy. Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet, etc.) when you visit a website. They are widely used to enhance the user experience, provide certain functionality, and collect information about your browsing activities.

Types of Cookies
We or third parties may use the following types of cookies on our Website:

Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the operation of our Website and enable you to navigate and use its features. They are usually set in response to your actions, such as logging in or filling out forms.

Analytical/Performance Cookies: These cookies allow us to recognize and count the number of visitors to our Website and understand how visitors interact with our Website. They help us analyze and improve the performance of our Website.

Functionality Cookies: These cookies enhance the functionality of our Website by remembering your preferences and choices. They enable personalized features, such as remembering your username or language selection.

Advertising/Targeting Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver advertisements that are relevant to you and your interests. They may also be used to limit the number of times you see an advertisement and measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns.

Why We Use Cookies
We use cookies on our Website for the following purposes:

-To ensure the proper functioning and security of our Website.
-To provide you with a personalized browsing experience.
-To analyze and improve the performance of our Website.
-To deliver targeted advertisements based on your interests.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences
Most web browsers are set to accept cookies by default. However, you can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. You can choose to accept or reject cookies or set your browser to prompt you before accepting a cookie. Please note that if you disable or reject cookies, certain features of our Website may not be available or function properly.

You can also manage your cookie preferences through third-party tools and platforms. For more information on how to control cookies, you can visit www.allaboutcookies.org.
