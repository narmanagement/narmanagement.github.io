About Us

Owner, Nicole A Reaves, has been in non-profit accounting for the last 20 years. She is a 2012 graduate of Devry University with a degree in Technical Management with a focus on Accounting. For the last 11+ years, she has successfully provided oversight and management of all financial functions for two $3M non-profit organizations.

Nicole is an artist at heart, previously attending school for fashion design. But she also holds a passion for accounting and finance. After findiing herself doing taxes and handling the accounting for local artists that "just want to create", Nicole formed NAR Management.  Our focus is on helping individual artists and business owners to successfully navigate their accounting and tax responsibilities so that they can focus 100% on their business!
