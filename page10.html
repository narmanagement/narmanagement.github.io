Mobirise is an offline app for Window and Mac to easily create small/medium websites, landing pages, online resumes and portfolios, promo sites for apps, events, services and products.

Is it good for me?

Mobirise is perfect for non-techies who are not familiar with the intricacies of web development and for designers who prefer to work as visually as possible, without fighting with code. Also great for pro-coders for fast prototyping and small customers projects.

Why Mobirise?

Frequently Asked Questions

 How can NAR Management help my business?

We offer accounting and bookkeeping, virtual CFO services, tax prep and advisory, and business development services, and more to help manage your finances, optimize your tax outcomes, provide strategic financial guidance, and support your business’ growth. Visit our What We Do page.

 Can you handle both personal and business accounting needs?

Yes, we provide accounting services for both personal and business needs, ensuring accurate financial management and tax compliance for individuals and businesses alike.

 Do you offer assistance with business funding and loans?

Absolutely! Our team will assess your business funding needs, explore financing options, and guide you through the application and documentation requirements. We'll work closely with you to prepare a compelling business case, financial projections, and other essential materials to increase your chances of obtaining funding. For businesses making $250,000+ annually, we can offer Working Capital Loans, Term Loans, and SBA 7(a) Loans.

 What tax services do you provide?

Tax Preparation: We handle the preparation and filing of various tax returns, such as individual income tax returns (Form 1040), non-profit tax returns (Form 990), partnership tax returns (Form 1065), and more.

Tax Advisory: We work closely with clients to develop effective tax strategies that minimize tax liabilities while maximizing potential deductions and credits. Our professionals analyze your financial situation and provide guidance on optimizing your tax position throughout the year.

IRS Audit Detection: Our program detects IRS audits and exams months in advance (in most cases), allowing the mitigation of penalties and interest, in addition to detecting IRS liens and levies. The program can also identify First Time Abatement opportunities which can result in additional refunds or offset of taxes owed.

IRS Representation: In case of tax audits, our experienced tax partners can provide representation and support during IRS or state tax authority inquiries. They can communicate with tax authorities on your behalf, helping to navigate the audit process and safeguard your interests. 

 How can you help with business entity formation?

We can assist you in establishing the appropriate legal structure for your business, such as LLC, corporation, or partnership. We handle the paperwork, compliance requirements, and guide you through the process efficiently.

 What is the process for getting started with NAR Management?

First, you should schedule a free 15 Minute Strategy Session to discuss your specific needs. From there, we can determine if we’ll be able to assist you and provide you with a proposal for services or if we can refer you to one of our strategic partners.

 What makes NAR Management different from other accounting firms?

At NAR Management, we offer a unique set of features and services that set us apart from other accounting and tax firms. We tailor our approach to meet the specific needs of our clients, providing customized solutions and personalized attention. Our commitment to understanding your unique circumstances ensures that our services are aligned with your goals and objectives.

We leverage advanced technology and automation tools to streamline our operations and enhance the efficiency of our services.

Transparency and education are fundamental to our client interactions. Unlike traditional accountants, we actively involve our clients in understanding their finances.

What truly sets us apart is our comprehensive expert network. We have established partnerships with professionals in diverse fields, including Human Resources, Insurance, Law, and Real Estate. Through these collaborations, we provide our clients with access to specialized advice and resources tailored to their specific needs.

By acting as a central hub that coordinates and integrates the expertise of different professionals, we offer our clients a seamless and holistic experience. This interconnected network fosters business growth, optimization, and sustainability, positioning us as trusted advisors who can navigate the complexities of running a business in diverse industries. 

 Do you offer remote or virtual services?

Yes! All of our services are virtual. We have been running a virtual firm since 2019.

 How do I schedule a consultation?

You can schedule a 15 Minute Strategy Session on our calendar here: booknar.10to8.com . During that session, we will talk about your current situation and what prompted you to call us. From there, we should be able to determine if we can provide you with a service proposal.

 Are your services customizable to meet our specific business needs?

Yes! We tailor our approach to meet the specific needs of our clients, providing customized solutions and personalized attention. Our commitment to understanding your unique circumstances ensures that our services are aligned with your goals and objectives.
